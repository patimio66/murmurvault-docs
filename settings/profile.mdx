Profile settings live at **Settings → Profile**. They control your account identity, not your vault contents.

## Name and email

Update your display name and email address. If you change your email, you may need to verify the new address before some actions are available again.

<Note>
  Your account email is also where top-secret reveal codes are sent. Keep it current and make sure you can receive mail there. See [Top-tier secrets](/vault/top-secrets).
</Note>

## Delete your account

The delete action on this page starts account deletion, a delayed and reversible flow. See [Account deletion](/settings/account) for the full process and grace period.
